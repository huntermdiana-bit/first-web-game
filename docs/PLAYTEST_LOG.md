# Playtest Log

## Latest Playtest

- Is the goal obvious? Yes. The menu and in-game text say to catch fish and avoid cucumbers.
- Are the controls clear? Yes. The menu shows Arrow Keys or A/D, and Space restarts after game over.
- Is it fun within 30 seconds? Yes for a first prototype. The player can move immediately, catch fish, react to diagonal drops, and hope for rare life hearts.
- Is losing understandable? Yes. Hitting cucumbers removes lives, and the game ends after 3 hits.
- Is the difficulty fair? Mostly. It starts gently, then adds speed and occasional diagonal movement as score increases.
- What is the single best next improvement? Add a tiny visual pop when catching fish, losing a life, or grabbing a heart.
