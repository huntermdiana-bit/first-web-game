# Game Seed

## Current Idea

A small arcade catching game called **Cat Fish Catch**.

The player controls a cat at the bottom of the screen. Fish fall from the sky and the cat tries to catch as many as possible. Cucumbers also fall, and the cat must avoid them.

## Core Fantasy

Be a quick, silly cat who loves fish but gets startled by cucumbers.

## Player Goal

Catch falling fish to earn points. Avoid falling cucumbers so the game does not end.

## Why It Should Be Fun

The idea is easy to understand right away: fish are good, cucumbers are bad. The player only has to move left and right, so the fun comes from quick reactions, chasing points, and trying again after a short round.

The first version should be playable in under 60 seconds and understandable within 5 seconds.
