# Mechanics

## Controls

- Move left with the left arrow key or A.
- Move right with the right arrow key or D.
- Restart with Space after losing.

## Core Loop

1. The cat starts at the bottom of the screen.
2. Fish, cucumbers, and rare life hearts fall from the top.
3. The player moves left and right.
4. Catching a fish adds score.
5. Touching a cucumber removes 1 life.
6. The player can restart and try for a higher score.

## Scoring Or Progress

- Each fish caught gives 1 point.
- The score is always visible.
- The best score can be shown later if it stays simple.

## Losing Or Failing

- The player loses 1 life when the cat touches a cucumber.
- The game ends after 3 cucumber hits.
- When the player loses, show a clear game over message and the final score.
- The game should explain that Space restarts.

## Helpful Items

- A rare green heart can fall after the player has scored a few points.
- Catching the heart restores 1 life, up to the 3-life maximum.
- Life hearts should stay rare so fish and cucumbers remain the main game.

## Difficulty Increase

- Start with slow falling objects.
- As the score increases, objects fall a little faster.
- Some objects start falling diagonally as the score increases.
- Add cucumbers slowly so the first few seconds feel fair.
- Keep the difficulty gentle for the first version.
